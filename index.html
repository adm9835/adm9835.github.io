About Andrew Moody

Andrew Moody is an online content creator who creates media for both live video and produced video.
Andrew is also skilled in inbound marketing and sales enablement including usage of web design, SEO, analytics, CRM software, social media marketing, blogging, and more.

Additionally, Andrew is very capable at coding after beginning self-teaching at age 11. He can utilize HTML, CSS, Java, Python, C#, as well as other tools including Unity, Blender, AutoDesk Maya, and more.
In his pastime, Andrew has become self-taught in photography, video production, video editing, and animating. He is capable of creating short form and long form video and animation pieces with ease using a variety of software including Sony Vegas, DaVinci Resolve, Anime Studio Pro, GIMP, and more.
With his undergraduate studies at University of North Carolina Wilmington, Andrew pursued and achieved a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communication Studies. Andrew is a highly skilled and capable communicator whose strong suits are in public speaking, strategic written communication, interpersonal communication, and teamwork.
First and foremost, Andrew's priorities include being an advocate for improving mental health education in the world and reducing stigmas against individuals with disorders and disabilities. He is also supportive of diversity, including uplifting individuals who experience discrimination such as women, people of color, individuals with LGBTQIA2+ identities, and more.
Andrew has experience working in marketing which included inspiring philanthropy, volunteerism, and improved education for youth.
When presented with sales goals, donation goals, customer satisfaction rates, and customer retention rates, Andrew has always been a consistent top performer within any capacity he has found himself in.
